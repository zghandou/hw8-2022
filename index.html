about me:

Hi, I'm Zeinab Ghandour! I'm currently a third year student studying user experinece design at the School of Information. Outside of my studies I'm an artist and I have enjoyed creating things for myself and others since I was four years old.

behind my art:

I like including "dreamy" elements to my art and find a lot of inspiration from classical paintings, as well as the media I enjoy. Clouds and muted/ pastel colors can usually be spotted in my art. I also find it more fun to make a variety of art so I use paints, markers, pencils, digital software (really anything) to bring my ideas to life. I like creating cartoonish art but I also love realism so there's a wide range !
