about:

hi! my name is zeinab ghandour! im currently a third year student studying user experience design at the school of information. outside of my studies i am an artist, with a love of various mediums! i have been into art since i learned how to old a pencil and it is what inspired me to have a creative career in design.

huh:

I love adding whimsical and dreamy elements to my art and find a lot of inspiration from classical paintings, as well as the media I enjoy. Clouds, stars, pastel colors, and such are trademarks of my style. I find it more fun to make a variety of art so I use paints, markers, pencils, digital software (really anything) to bring my ideas to life. I like cartoonish art but I also love elements of realism.
